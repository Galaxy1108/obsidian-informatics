### 基本公式

$$
\begin{aligned}
	H_n &= \frac{1}{n+1}C_{2n}^n \\
	    &= \frac{(2n)!}{(n+1)!n!}
\end{aligned}
$$
$$
\begin{array}{rcl}
	H_n=C_{2n}^{n}-C_{2n}^{n+1} & \mbox{for} \ \ n \ge 1
\end{array}
$$
$$
H_0=1 \ \ \ \  \mbox{and} \ \ \ \  C_{n+1}=\sum_{\mathclap{i=0}}^{n}H_iH_{n-i} \ \ \ \ \mbox{for} \ n\ge0
$$
$$
H_0=1 \ \ \ \ \mbox{and} \ \ \ \ H_{n+1}=\frac{2(2n+1)}{n+2}H_n
$$
